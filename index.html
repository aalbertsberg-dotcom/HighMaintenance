Our Story

Locally Owned. Locally Loved.

High Maintenance Hair Salon is a locally owned and operated Gulf Coast institution, built on the belief that every client deserves to leave feeling exceptional. Our talented team of stylists, colorists, and beauty artists brings both skill and heart to every single chair.

From everyday cuts to pageant-stage glamour, we've been trusted by thousands of Gulfport clients and counting. With nearly a five-star rating across hundreds of reviews, we let our work speak for itself.
